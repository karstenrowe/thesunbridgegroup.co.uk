History & credentials

The Sunbridge Group have been building quality homes throughout West Yorkshire for over 40 years, from the Yorkshire Dales to the principal cities, industrial towns and villages, providing varied and interesting developments to suit all types of purchaser.

Approach

A high degree of personal involvement by our management is considered essential in all transactions. Our aim is to find your dream home.

We confidently look forward to providing many more beautiful homes for generations to come.

Services

  • Modern houses bought & sold.
  • Houses & apartments to let.
  • Building land always required.
  • Joint venture schemes considered.
  • Building finance available.
