Savile Road, Elland From £140,000

A Stunning conversion of a former office building into 7 three bedroom and 1 four bedroom town houses, and 1 new build three bedroom town house. All covered by the LABC 10 Year Warranty Scheme.

Located on the outskirts of Elland Town centre the development is ideally positioned for a wide range of local amenities and boasts excellent commuter links to Halifax, Brighouse, Huddersfield along with access to Junction 24 of the M62 motorway.

Property features

  • A selection of 3/4 bedroom townhouses.
  • Two allocated parking bays per property.
  • Large patio and enclosed garden, ideal for families.
  • Each property has a ground floor cloakroom, family bathroom and en-suite facilities.
  • Options on kitchen unit styles and bathroom finish.
  • Bi-folding doors to the lounge, creating light living space and direct access to the spacious patio and enclosed garden.

Directions

From Elland Town Centre proceed along the Victoria Road, take the second turning left into Elsinore Avenue. Continue along Elsinore Avenue until the roads bears left into South Parade and the development can be found on the right and is identified by our For Sale board. View on Google Maps.
